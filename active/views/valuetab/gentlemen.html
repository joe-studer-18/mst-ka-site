You are A KA

The ideals of Kappa Alpha Order can be best represented by our two mottos: Excelsior and Dieu et les Dames, with the former being Latin for “excellence in all things”, and the latter being French for “Of God and the Ladies”. The Beta Alpha Chapter at Missouri S&T takes these mottos a step further, which can be clearly seen by anyone who walks our halls, as the phrase “May God, Chivalry, Brotherhood, and Excellence in all things be our guide.” are inscribed on plaques at every major exit to our house. Every day, on our way to class, as we exit the house we touch the plaques to remind ourselves of how we, as Gentlemen of Kappa Alpha Order, should uphold ourselves on campus and in our daily lives.
